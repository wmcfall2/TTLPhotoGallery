Tindog Tabang Leyteno Photo Gallery

The Tindog Tabang Leyteno (TTL) program is a three-year USD 20 million program funded through the American Red Cross (ARC) and working in partnership with the Philippine Red Cross (PRC) towards the promotion of community resilience in areas affected by Typhoon Haiyan. The program strives to promote community resilience through capacity building interventions at the PRC and community level and through the provision of cash and in-kind resources at the household and community level. Although the program is implemented in an integrated fashion, program activities generally fall within Shelter, Livelihoods, Water and Sanitation (WatSan), Health, or Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) interventions. This page highlights the dedicated and focused work of TTL staff/volunteers in the implementation of the TTL program.
